The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Each of us has an unlimited capacity for creativity and innovation. Poverty is not created by the poor; it is imposed by the system we have built. Bangladesh, despite its challenges, is filled with untapped potential, and its people have the courage and strength to overcome any obstacle. We must rethink our institutions, our policies, and our values to ensure that no one is left behind. By empowering the poor and giving them access to credit, education, and opportunity, we can build a world without poverty—a world of dignity, equality, and shared prosperity for everyone."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh

Bangladesh Launches New Satellite

Date: 05/01/2025 Time: 10.25 PM

Bangabandhu-1, Bangladesh's first satellite, launched on May 12, 2018, revolutionized the nation's communication and broadcasting. It ensures improved internet access, especially in remote areas, enhances disaster management, and reduces reliance on foreign satellites. Named after Sheikh Mujibur Rahman, it represents Bangladesh’s leap into space technology and digital advancement.
